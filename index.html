ABOUT US

I have developed the version 2 of this project solely out of my passion and interest to refine, resolve and debug issues from version 1. The new version 2 features a more-secure verification mechanism to recognise fake documents, even in a situation where the original file is lost, or its link is not accessible.

A group of passionate and goal-driven students developed the version 1 of this product / software as a part of our Bachelor's degree Project. We made use of the Blockchain Technology to power our application, where we recorded transaction data and generated NFT Certificates from the inputs given by user.
